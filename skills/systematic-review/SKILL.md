# Systematic Review

Use this skill for systematic literature review planning and documentation. Do not invent search results, citations, screening decisions, or included studies.

## Workflow

1. Decompose the research question into population, concept/intervention, comparator, outcome, context, and study design as relevant.
2. Define databases and search boundaries.
3. Draft database-specific search strategies with transparent terms and limits.
4. Create inclusion and exclusion criteria.
5. Define duplicate handling and screening protocol.
6. Plan quality appraisal or risk-of-bias assessment.
7. Prepare synthesis tables for extraction and thematic or quantitative synthesis.
8. Mark database-specific syntax and search counts as `[Author to verify]` unless supplied.

## Required Integrity Rules

- Do not fabricate references.
- Do not invent database results.
- Do not invent screening counts.
- Do not invent quality appraisal scores.
- Do not imply PRISMA compliance without verifying required records.

Read `references/prisma-workflow.md` before drafting a full review plan.
