# Journal Submission Checker

Use this skill to prepare or audit a manuscript submission package.

## Workflow

1. Identify journal, article type, submission stage, and provided instructions.
2. Check required files: manuscript, title page, cover letter, figures, tables, supplementary files, highlights, graphical abstract, declarations, and response documents when applicable.
3. Audit required statements: ethics, consent, data availability, conflict of interest, funding, author contributions, acknowledgements, and AI-use disclosure.
4. Check journal scope fit based on provided journal aims or author-supplied rationale.
5. Mark all journal-specific uncertainties as `[Author to verify]`.

## Output

Produce:

- package checklist
- missing information list
- statement audit
- cover letter fit notes
- author verification checklist

Do not claim compliance with journal rules unless the relevant instructions were provided and checked.
