## Status Values

- `addressed`: A manuscript change directly addresses the concern.
- `partially addressed`: The concern was addressed within reasonable scope, but not fully resolved.
- `clarified`: The response explains an issue without a major manuscript change.
- `not changed with justification`: No manuscript change was made, and the rationale is stated.
- `author verification needed`: The claim needs confirmation from the author or manuscript.
