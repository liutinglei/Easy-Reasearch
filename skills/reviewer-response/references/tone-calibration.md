# Tone Calibration

Use language that is respectful, concise, and specific.

## Prefer

- "Thank you for pointing this out."
- "We have revised the relevant paragraph to clarify..."
- "We agree that the previous wording could be clearer."
- "We have added a limitation statement..."

## Avoid

- defensive explanations
- excessive praise
- claims that reviewers misunderstood without evidence
- vague statements such as "we improved the manuscript"
- promises that are not supported by the revised text

When disagreeing, state the reason and any limited change made to improve clarity.
